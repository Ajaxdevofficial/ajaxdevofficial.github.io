Introducing the ProperT Token

The first
token-back rewards program

Buyers can purchase any good or service through ForumPay-supported merchants and businesses. ProperT holders will automatically get a 5% token-back reward on the purchase price. ProperT tokens function like any other currency and can be used to transact everyday goods and services; a critical differentiating factor in the crypto space!

*Tokens equal to 5% of purchases made with the Token are awarded subject to a weekly cap calculated at an annual rate of 10% of each years new release.

Utility

For buyers

ProperT is a solution that is native to crypto. It enables crypto-holders to convert their crypto-currency through the ProperT dashboard and make purchases in ForumPay-supported merchants and businesses. ProperT works like any other currency that can be used to buy goods, services or real estate, with the additional benefit of receiving a 5% token-back reward for every purchase made. ProperT is the first token-back reward program and has been designed in a way that user experience is easy, fast and secure.

For merchants

Offer a rewards program that rewards your customer with a 5% token-back for any purchase at no cost to you. Stand out among your competition and offer this unique reward to attract new customers.
