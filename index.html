Quick start guide

Download the Trust Wallet or Metamask from your app store.
Open the Trust Wallet app and create an account.
Once you are in, tap the + icon to add a new token to your wallet.

Input the Elon Gold token address under Contract Address:
0x0xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
Tap Save in the top right corner.

Elon Gold token (EGT) will appear in your wallet.
Tap Receive to create your wallet address.

Once your address is created, you can deposit or receive Elon Gold Tokens (EGT) to your Trust Wallet.
Copy your Trust Wallet address.
Important: This is the address where you will receive your tokens.

Purchase EGT using PancakeSwap: Go to Dapps pancakeswap.finance and connect your wallet
